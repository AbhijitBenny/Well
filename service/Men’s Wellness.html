Is it painful?

What is Shockwave Therapy?
A non-invasive therapy that uses sound waves to stimulate blood flow and tissue repair.

How effective is it?
Many patients report significant improvements after a few sessions.

What are PDE5 inhibitors?
Medications like Viagra and Cialis improve blood flow to support erectile function.
