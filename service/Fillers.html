Common Questions


What is Restylane used for?
Restylane is commonly used to smooth wrinkles and enhance volume in areas like lips and cheeks.

How long do Restylane results last?
Results typically last 6-18 months, depending on the treated area.

Are there side effects of Restylane?
Mild swelling, redness, or bruising at the injection site are common but temporary.

What makes Juvederm unique?
Juvederm is versatile and suitable for lips, cheeks, and deeper wrinkles.

How soon will I see results?
Results are immediate and improve over the next few days.

Is Juvederm safe?
Yes, Juvederm is FDA-approved and widely regarded as safe.
