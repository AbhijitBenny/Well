Common Questions


What is Botox used for?
Botox reduces wrinkles and fine lines caused by facial expressions.

How long do Botox results last?
Results typically last 3-6 months.

Are there side effects of Botox?
Side effects are minimal, such as slight redness or swelling at the injection site.

How is Dysport different from Botox?
Dysport spreads more easily, making it ideal for larger treatment areas.

What areas can Dysport treat?
Common areas include forehead lines and crow's feet.

Is Dysport safe?
Yes, it’s FDA-approved and safe when administered by professionals.

What is Jeuveau?
Jeuveau is a modern injectable for smoothing wrinkles, often called “Newtox.”

How long does Jeuveau take to show results?
Results typically appear within 2-3 days.

How long do Jeuveau results last?
Effects last approximately 4-6 months.

What makes Daxxify unique?
Daxxify offers longer-lasting results compared to other injectables.

How long does Daxxify last?
Results can last up to 6-9 months.

Who is a good candidate for Daxxify?
Anyone looking for a long-term solution to wrinkles and fine lines.
