Common Questions


What is Morpheus8?
Morpheus8 is a treatment that combines microneedling and radiofrequency to stimulate deep skin rejuvenation.

What skin concerns does it address?
It helps improve skin laxity, texture, and the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.

How many sessions are needed?
Most patients see results after 1-3 sessions, depending on their goals.

What is RF Microneedling?
RF Microneedling uses radiofrequency energy and microneedles to tighten and rejuvenate the skin.

Is there downtime after treatment?
Downtime is minimal, with most patients resuming normal activities within 1-2 days.

What results can I expect?
Expect firmer, smoother skin with reduced wrinkles and improved texture.
