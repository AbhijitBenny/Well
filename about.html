About The Wellness Code

Empowering individuals to unlock their healthiest selves

Your Path to Wellness Begins Here

Welcome to The Wellness Code

At The Wellness Code, we believe in unlocking your true potential through empowerment, education, and partnership.
Your health and wellness journey is a collaborative process, and we’re here to guide you every step of the way.

Our Approach

We take a comprehensive, personalized, and proactive approach to health and wellness.
Rather than just treating symptoms, we focus on the whole person.
By understanding your unique health history, family background, and personal wellness goals,
we develop customized treatment plans tailored just for you.

Whether you want to feel healthier, look younger, or optimize your overall well-being,
our dedicated medical professionals are committed to helping you achieve your goals.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: To help you feel better, live better, and be better.

Why Choose Us?

Experienced Professionals

Our team consists of highly skilled and knowledgeable medical experts specializing in a variety of treatments

Holistic & Cutting-Edge Solutions

From traditional medical services to natural, innovative treatments, we offer options that are effective, evidence-based, and not yet widely available.

Patient Empowerment

We educate and inform, so you can make confident, informed decisions about your health. At The Wellness Code, you’re always in the driver’s seat.

Meet the Team

Ben Graham

PA-C is a Board-Certified Physician Assistant with over 9 years of experience in Emergency Medicine, Urgent Care, Orthopedic Surgery, HRT, and Aesthetics. He holds advanced degrees in Physician Assistant Studies and Clinical Psychology. Known for his calm demeanor and clear communication, he began his career in a busy ER, honing critical care skills. Based in Dallas, TX, he enjoys basketball, weightlifting, and learning languages.



Dr. Rubin Simon

MD is an experienced internal medicine specialist with over 14 years in the medical field. He graduated from the University of Texas Health San Antonio School of Medicine in 2009. As the Medical Director, Dr. Simon combines his medical expertise with a commitment to holistic well-being. Outside of his professional work, he is a family man who enjoys fishing and fitness. His balanced approach to life reflects the comprehensive care provided at Wellness Code, where he leads with both medical excellence and compassion.
